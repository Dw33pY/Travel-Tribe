Meet The Tribe

Our Story

Passionate about connecting people to the soul of Africa.

Travel Tribe was born from a shared love for wild places, authentic encounters, and the belief that travel should transform both the traveler and the destination.

Founded in 2018 by a collective of Kenyan guides, conservationists, and hospitality experts, we set out to create a different kind of travel company—one that prioritizes community, sustainability, and deep immersion over cookie-cutter tours.

Every journey we design is a collaboration with local communities. We work with Maasai elders to offer respectful cultural visits, partner with marine biologists in Zanzibar to protect coral reefs, and employ guides who grew up in the very landscapes they now interpret for our guests.

Our name, Travel Tribe, reflects the bonds we forge—between travelers and places, between our team and our partners, and among the travelers themselves. Whether you're watching the Great Migration from a hot air balloon, sharing stories around a campfire, or learning to cook Swahili curry in a Stone Town kitchen, you become part of our extended family.

We're small enough to care deeply, and experienced enough to deliver flawlessly. Let's explore together.
